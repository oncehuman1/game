IS YOUR GEAR SECURE?

And 6 things you need to know

1 . Is Once Human truly Free-to-Play or is it Pay-to-Win?
Once Human is 100% Free-to-Play. Our shop is strictly cosmetic. You cannot buy power, speed, or advantage. Strategy and skill are the only currencies here.
2 . Can I survive the apocalypse as a Solo player?
Absolutely. While you can join Hives (Warbands), the entire story and world are optimized for solo survivors. You choose how you face the Stardust.
3 . Will my PC handle the New Weird?
We’ve optimized the Stardust Engine to run on a wide range of hardware. From mid-range laptops to high-end rigs, the apocalypse looks stunning.
4 . Does my progress reset? How do Seasons work?
Once Human uses a Seasonal System to keep the meta fresh. While some world progress resets to keep the challenge alive, your blueprints, gear, and cosmetics stay with you forever.
5 . Is this a PvP or PvE game?
Both. You choose your server type. Prefer a terrifying story? Choose PvE. Want high-stakes territory wars? Choose PvP. Your playstyle, your rules.
6. Can I play with friends on Mobile or Console?
Once Human is currently on PC, with Mobile and Console versions in active development. Cross-progression will ensure your character follows you everywhere.
